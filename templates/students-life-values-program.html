Beginning in Kindergarten and all through high school we have implemented a program called “The 6 Pillars of Character”. By working on six basic values we help form our students

Our objective is to create a harmonious school environment, to prevent antisocial behaviours and to encouragepositive attitudes, responsible decision making and autonomy.

Activities related to each value are performed with the students. The value changes every two months, which allowstime to work on the value by story telling, songs, theatrical representations, games and special assignments.
