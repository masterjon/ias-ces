Model United Nations

Model UN is a seminar class during which students study the history and functions of the United Nations. Most students then participate in a conference that takes place in another state or country.

Other Observations

a) In 12th grade students are assigned to math classes according to their math skills and preference.
b) During their senior year, students can choose between physics and ecology.
c) Students in grades 9-12 choose electives each semester. In the past these electives have included drawing, painting, woodworking, chess, computer drawing, drama, chorus, Intro to German, and SAT prep.
d) Orientation is a class in which students explore their personalities, career options, test-taking skills, etc.
